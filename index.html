Frequently Asked Questions

When is the best time to visit Thalassery?

The best time to visit Thalassery is from October to February, when the weather is pleasant for outdoor activities.

What are some popular things to do in Thalassery?

Some popular activities with children include Overbury's Folly, Tagore Park, and Thalassery Fort.

What are some attractions in Thalassery?

Thalassery has a variety of attractions, including shopping, local history, day tours, and transfers.

What is Thalassery commonly well known for?

Thalassery is nicknamed the city of three Cs, which refers to cake, cricket, and circus. The first bakery in Kerala was established in Thalassery, and cricket was first played there.

What are some places to visit in Thalassery?

Some places to visit include Mahe River, Odathil Palli, and Thalassery Fort.

How long does it take to get food from a restaurant?

Fast food dishes usually take 15–30 minutes to prepare

Are inexpensive restaurants hygienic?

Yes, the plates, spoons, and other cutlery are all clean

What dishes are popular in Thalassery?

Biryani, fried Kallumakkayas, and dum for the biryani.
